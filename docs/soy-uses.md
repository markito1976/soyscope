## 1. Adhesives & Binders

**Market**: $2.3B soy adhesive market projected by 2028

### Wood Panel Adhesives
- **PureBond** (Columbia Forest Products, 2005) -- formaldehyde-free soy protein adhesive for hardwood plywood, largest consumer of industrial soy protein
- **Soyad** (Solenis, 2004) -- soy flour + PAE crosslinker system for plywood/OSB, achieves D4 water resistance
- **Heartland BioComposites** (2001) -- soy protein binder for strawboard panels
- Soy protein concentrate + epoxy crosslinker adhesives for engineered wood
- Soy flour + polyamidoamine-epichlorohydrin (PAE) systems

### Laminating & Packaging Adhesives
- Soy protein-based laminating adhesives for food packaging
- Soy-casein hybrid adhesives for labeling and carton sealing
- Hot-melt adhesives from dimer fatty acids (soy-derived)

## 2. Coatings, Paints & Surface Treatments

**Market**: $7.2B alkyd resin market (2023); 1.5B+ lbs/yr soy oil consumed in US coatings

### Alkyd Resins
- Soy oil alkyd coatings -- dominant bio-based coating resin platform
- Modified soy alkyds for architectural and industrial paints
- Short-oil, medium-oil, and long-oil alkyd formulations

### Advanced Coatings
- Acrylated epoxidized soybean oil (AESO) for UV-curable coatings
- Soy-based polyurethane dispersions for wood and metal
- Soy oil epoxy-amine thermoset coatings
- Vermont Natural Coatings (2009) -- soy-based wood floor finishes

### Printing Inks
- **SoySeal** (American Soybean Association, 1987) -- certification program
- 95% of US daily newspaper inks are soy-based
- Lithographic, heatset, sheetfed offset, flexographic soy inks
- Lower VOC emissions vs petroleum inks; easier de-inking for recycling

- **BiOH** (Cargill, 2008) -- soy polyols for flexible/rigid PU foams
- **Agrol** (BioBased Systems, 2006) -- spray foam insulation polyols

  - Replaces phthalate plasticizers (DEHP, DINP)
- ESBO in food-contact PVC (cling wrap, gaskets, bottle cap liners)

### Bioplastics
- Soy protein + PLA blend films for packaging
- Ford Motor Company soy protein composites (heritage from 1941 "soybean car")
- Soy-filled polypropylene and polyethylene composites

## 4. Lubricants & Metalworking Fluids

**Market**: $3.5B bio-lubricant market (2025)

### Dielectric/Transformer Fluids
- **Envirotemp FR3** (Cargill, 1998) -- soy natural ester for power transformers; 3M+ units installed globally
- Superior fire safety (flash point 360C vs 160C for mineral oil)
- IEEE C57.147 standard for natural ester fluids

### Industrial Lubricants
- **BioSOY** (Biosynthetic Technologies, 2012) -- estolide lubricants
- Soy-based hydraulic fluids for construction/forestry equipment
- Chainsaw bar oil, rail flange lubricant (total-loss applications)
- Two-cycle engine oil for marine outboard motors
- Gear oils, compressor oils meeting AGMA specifications
- Renewable Lubricants Inc. (2005) -- soy gear/compressor oils

### Metalworking
- Soy-based cutting fluids, quenchants, mold release agents
- Metalworking coolants with improved biodegradability

## 5. Energy & Biofuels

**Market**: $56.5B global biodiesel market (2024)

### Biodiesel (FAME)
- Soy oil = #1 US biodiesel feedstock
- B20 (20% blend) most common commercial concentration
- B100 (neat) for off-road and marine diesel
- Transesterification of soy triglycerides to fatty acid methyl esters (FAME)

### Renewable Diesel (HVO)
- **Diamond Green Diesel** / **Neste** / **Marathon** -- hydrotreated vegetable oil
- Market: $61.8B by 2030
- Drop-in replacement for petroleum diesel

### Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF)
- **HEFA-SPK** process from soy oil via hydroprocessing
- World Energy, Neste -- commercial SAF producers
- ASTM D7566 certified blending up to 50%

### Glycerin Byproducts
- Crude glycerin from biodiesel → propylene glycol, epichlorohydrin
- Solvay/Dow bio-glycerol conversion plants (2007+)

## 8. Chemicals & Solvents

### Methyl Soyate (Soy Methyl Ester)
**Market**: $1.64B by 2030
- **SoyGold** (AG Environmental Products, 1995) -- industrial solvent
- **VertecBio Gold** (Vertec BioSolvents, 2002) -- high-purity solvent
- **SoyStrip** (Franmar Chemical, 1997) -- paint/coating remover
- Applications: parts washing, paint stripping, ink cleaning, asphalt release
- Replaces mineral spirits, MEK, acetone, methylene chloride

### Azelaic Acid
**Market**: $929M (2024)
- C9 dicarboxylic acid from soy oil ozonolysis
- Produced by Emery Oleochemicals, Matrica (ENI-Novamont JV)
- Applications: Nylon 6,9 monomer, plasticizers, lubricants, cosmetics (acne treatment)

### Dimer Fatty Acids
**Market**: $823M (2025)
- Dimerized C18 soy fatty acids (C36 dimer)
- Producers: Croda, Kraton, Arizona Chemical
- Applications: polyamide hot-melt adhesives, printing ink resins, corrosion inhibitors

### Oleochemicals & Intermediates
- Soy fatty acid fractionation for surfactants, alkyd resins, rubber chemicals
- Industrial soy lecithin as emulsifier, release agent, wetting agent (Cargill, ADM, Bunge)
- Bio-glycerol conversion to epichlorohydrin, propylene glycol (Solvay, Dow)

### Soy Molasses
- Byproduct of soy protein concentrate (SPC) production
- Carbon source for ethanol, citric acid, enzyme fermentation
- Emerging platform chemical feedstock
